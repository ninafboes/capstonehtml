Case Study

What is Well Done:

Creating programs to get more girls into STEM can help improve their confidence in their abilities, ideally increasing the retention of girls in STEM studies.
Explanation:
This case study has many donors and partners to help them in their goal of increasing girl's engagement and interest in STEM fields
Explanation:
The program creates summer camps and after-school programs to reach their goal

How Tech is Used:

The website uses technology in a beneficial way to interact with users but it could be improved a bit by increasing the promotion of the programs.
Explanation:
Their website is used to accept donations, provide information, and signups for programs. There is also technology in the cirriculum used to help teach the girls.
Explanation:
Technology can be used to further improve the effectiveness by helping with promotion so more girls get access to the programs they are providing.
Implication:
I like that there are different options- camps, after-school programs, smaller group sessions, etc. However, many of the programs linked cost money or were slightly expensive. I can improve the accessibility of the case study. I would ideally link resources to free/cheaper services.

Implication:

I felt like the website lacked enough human interaction. I would like to create a place where users can interact. They would give advice to each other or even provide resources that they have found useful in their journey as a woman in STEM.
